Searching for Food

They were batteling with storms, they flee from predators, they fought enemies and they travelled unstopabbly towards the furtherest end of the world were there was everything they they could ever have fantasised for.

Under the palm tree, both of them were carried away with the beautiful melody produced from their ukulele. They ate as much as they wanted, they swam as much as they wanted, they were enjoying themselves to the fullest of their desire.

"Jerry, wake up! You'll be late for classes", squeaked Jerry's mom.

It was Monday morning and Jerry cant wait to tell his friend of the wanderful adventrous dream that he had about them. Quickly got himself ready, he went to school.
