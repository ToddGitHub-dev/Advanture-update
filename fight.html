Fight

"Hunt each other it is", cried Tom.

Jerry was nervous about Tom’s intension. He was about to protest but he realised that it was too late. Tom had his claws out of his paws already, jumping like a giant beast towards Jerry.

After some seconds, Jerry realised that he was running away from a monster, once was a friend. With his clever tricks, he fitted himself in a niche on a wooden wall that Tom couldn’t reach and Tom and Jerry were never to be friends again ever after.

"Tom, wake up! You'll be late for church", howled Tom's dad.

It was Sunday morning and Tom cant wait to tell his friend of the spooky adventrous dream that he had about them. Quickly got himself ready, he went to school.
